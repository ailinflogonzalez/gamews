What Is an MMO?

Massively multiplayer online games, explained

It’s a game where you play with lots of other players online to achieve the goal you would normally achieve in a single player game. However it is team-oriented and even though you can play on your own that’s not always the case. Some mmos have a mandatory team-oriented play-through. You also can’t play without an internet connection. In that game you can communicate with other player as you would in real life, primarily in english and use this to beat the game. Also there is no actual beating of them game each player considers his own goal of the game. The game is actually infinite and is focused around making friends and player communication.
